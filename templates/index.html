📋 MyC-Lite Lexical Rules Reference

Prefix Rule (Rule 1 & 6)

Identifiers must start with:
v_ (Variables), fn_ (Functions), or c_ (Constants).
Example: v_total, c_PI

Length & Constraints (Rule 3 & 4)

Max length is 20 characters.
Standalone prefixes (v_, fn_, c_) are completely forbidden.

Readability Warning (Rule 5)

Identifiers with payloads under 3 characters (excluding prefix) trigger a Static Analysis Warning.

Custom Keywords (Rule 2)

Standard types + custom additions:
begin, end, display, input, repeat, until
